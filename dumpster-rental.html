Not Sure Which You Need?

Dumpster Rental vs. Junk Removal

Both get the job done. Here's how to pick the right one for your situation.

Dumpster Rental
Best when you're doing the work yourself
  • You control the pace — fill it over days or weeks
  • Great for renovation projects with ongoing debris
  • Contractors and DIYers working a job site
  • Whole-house cleanouts you want to sort yourself
Junk Removal
Best when you want us to handle everything
  • We show up, carry it out, and load the truck
  • You don't lift a finger or manage a timeline
  • Single items up to full property cleanouts
  • Same-day results — space is clear the same day
What Can Go In

Fill It With Almost Anything

A few things we can't take for safety reasons — everything else, load it up.

✓ Accepted

  • Household junk & furniture
  • Construction & renovation debris
  • Concrete, brick & dirt
  • Lumber & drywall
  • Roofing materials & shingles
  • Appliances (most)
  • Yard waste & landscaping debris
  • Scrap metal
  • Electronics & e-waste
  • Cardboard & packing materials

✕ Not Accepted

  • Paint & paint cans
  • Household chemicals & solvents
  • Propane tanks
  • Asbestos-containing materials
  • Medical waste & sharps
  • Gasoline & fuel
  • Batteries (some exceptions)
  • Firearms & ammunition
